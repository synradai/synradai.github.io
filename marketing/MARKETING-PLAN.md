# Marketing Plan — Safe Intelligence (Marketer agent, 2026-07-08)

*Pricing corrected to the board-approved framing: early supporter rate
**$29/month for 6 months, then $49/month**.*

**Market read:** the buyer is real and Trent knows them personally. The personal
network IS the go-to-market for beta. No cold channels until 3–5 people can say
"I use this and it works."

## Three phases

### Phase 1 — Beta (now, weeks 1–3)
Goal: 10 users, zero payment, maximum learning. Not marketing — conversations.

### Phase 2 — Pre-launch warmup (weeks 2–3, parallel with legal)
Goal: small waitlist while legal closes. One LinkedIn post, no selling — just
signalling you exist.

### Phase 3 — Public launch (the day legal/insurance gates close)
Stripe live, early-supporter pricing out, beta social proof in hand. This is
when you sell.

## This week — night by night

- **Night 1 (30 min):** list 10 people you'd trust to give honest feedback —
  people who'll say it's broken, not people who'll be nice. Rank by trust ×
  compliance pain. Top 10 get personal messages. No broadcasting.
- **Night 2 (20 min):** send the first 3–4 beta invites (template below).
  One at a time — WhatsApp/text/LinkedIn DM, never a group.
- **Night 3 (20 min):** free Mailchimp or Brevo account → one list:
  "Safe Intelligence waitlist." Everyone who responds positively goes in.
  Even 30 names is enough to generate first paying customers.
- **Night 4 (30 min):** draft LinkedIn post #1 (below). Save as draft —
  publish in week 2 once 1–2 beta users are active and can comment.

## The beta invite (personal DM, one person at a time)

> Hey [name], I've been building something on the side that I think you'll find
> genuinely useful — an AI tool specifically for WHS work. Site rounds, incident
> reports, field leadership reports — it drafts them from a voice note or quick
> description, and it knows Australian regs.
>
> I've got it to a point where it actually works well. I'm letting a small
> handful of people (max 10) use it free before I open it up, and I want real
> feedback from people who actually do this work, not just people who'll say
> it's great.
>
> No cost, no commitment. You'd just use it on a real swing, tell me what's
> wrong with it, and what it does well.
>
> Interested? I'll send you the link and a 5-minute walkthrough.

Don't send the link until they say yes — make them take one step first.
