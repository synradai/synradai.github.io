Privacy Policy

Effective 29 June 2026 · Synrad AI

Terms of Service · Open the app

This Privacy Policy explains how Synrad AI ("we", "us") handles personal information when you use the Safe Intelligence app. We handle personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What we collect

  • Account details — your name and email address.
  • Content you enter — shift notes, site observations, incident and field-leadership reports, daily logs, photos, and questions you ask the AI assistant ("Gaz").
  • Usage & technical data — basic information needed to run and secure the service.
  • Billing data — handled by Stripe; we do not store your card details.

Sensitive information — your responsibility

Safety records can contain other people's personal information. Only enter what is necessary, and use the app's de-identification tools where you can. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to record information about other people.

How we use it

To provide and maintain the app, generate AI safety guidance and reports, keep your data backed up and isolated to your account, process your subscription, and improve the service.

AI processing and overseas disclosure

To generate responses and reports, content you submit is sent to our AI provider, Anthropic, which processes it on servers in the United States. By using the AI features you consent to this overseas disclosure. We send only what is needed and do not sell your data.

Where your data is stored

Your account and content are stored in our database (Supabase), encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Each customer's data is isolated by row-level security. Two-factor authentication is available.

Service providers

  • Supabase — database, authentication, backend functions.
  • Anthropic — AI processing (USA).
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • GitHub Pages — app hosting.

Retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your content in the app, and you can ask us to delete your account and associated data by emailing us.

Access, correction and complaints

You can access and correct most information directly in the app. To request access, correction, deletion, or to make a privacy complaint, email synradai@outlook.com. If you are not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

Data breaches

If a data breach occurs that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the OAIC in line with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

Changes

We may update this policy. We'll change the effective date above and, for material changes, let you know in the app.
