# Government Funding & Grants — Safe Intelligence (researched 2026-07-08)

Ranked by realistic fit for a WA solo founder, pre-revenue safety-tech product.

## 1. WA Innovation Booster Grant (IBG) — THE one to chase ★

- **What:** Up to **$50,000** for WA early-stage founders/startups to "refine,
  test, protect, validate and certify" an innovation. Funded by the $40M New
  Industries and Innovation Fund (2025-29), run by Dept of Energy and Economic
  Diversification (DEED).
- **Fit:** Built for exactly this — an innovative WA SME commercialising a
  product. Eligible spend maps to our real needs: legal review, security
  certification, trademark, UX, market validation.
- **Co-contribution:** 20% cash (15% for regional/female/First Nations founded —
  Mandurah sits in the Peel region, which is classed as regional WA under state
  definitions; confirm with DEED whether the business qualifies as regional).
- **Timing:** The 2026 round opened 10 March and **closed 30 March 2026** — we
  missed it. **Next round: ~March 2027.** Applications are short, competitive
  windows — be ready in February 2027.
- **How to win it:** by then have: ABN (+ likely Pty Ltd), lawyer-reviewed
  docs, 10+ beta users, first paying customers, and letters of support from
  safety advisors. Traction is what separates winners.
- Links: [WA.gov.au program page](https://www.wa.gov.au/organisation/department-of-energy-and-economic-diversification/new-industries-and-innovation-fund-innovation-booster-grant) ·
  [2026 round announcement](https://www.wa.gov.au/government/announcements/innovation-booster-grant-2026-applications-now-open) ·
  [business.gov.au listing](https://business.gov.au/grants-and-programs/innovation-booster-grant-wa)

## 2. R&D Tax Incentive (federal) — real money, but needs a Pty Ltd

- **What:** Refundable tax offset of **43.5%** of eligible R&D spend for
  companies under $20M turnover. Software development with genuine technical
  uncertainty qualifies.
- **Catch #1:** **Companies only — sole traders are NOT eligible.** (Another
  point for the incorporate-now column; raise with the lawyer/accountant.)
- **Catch #2:** Minimum ~$20k of notional R&D deductions, and a founder's
  unpaid nights don't count — the company must actually incur the cost (e.g.
  pay you a wage, pay contractors, API/infra costs).
- **Verdict:** Not free money today; becomes relevant the year Synrad AI is a
  Pty Ltd with real R&D expenditure. Register activities with AusIndustry
  within 10 months of financial year end.
- Links: [business.gov.au — eligibility](https://business.gov.au/grants-and-programs/research-and-development-tax-incentive/check-if-you-are-eligible-for-the-randd-tax-incentive) ·
  [ATO overview](https://www.ato.gov.au/businesses-and-organisations/income-deductions-and-concessions/incentives-and-concessions/research-and-development-tax-incentive/about-the-r-d-tax-incentive-program)

## 3. Industry Growth Program (federal) — later, at real traction

- **What:** Grants **$50k–$250k** (early commercialisation) up to **$100k–$5M**
  (growth) for innovative SMEs in National Reconstruction Fund priority areas —
  "value-add in resources" is a plausible fit for mining-safety tech.
- **Catches:** must first go through their Advisory Service; requires ABN,
  income-tax-paying entity **and GST registration** (we're not GST-registered);
  one 2026 source reports the program **paused to new applications** — verify
  status when relevant.
- **Verdict:** Park until there are paying customers and a growth story.
- Links: [industry.gov.au](https://www.industry.gov.au/science-technology-and-innovation/industry-innovation/industry-growth-program) ·
  [business.gov.au](https://business.gov.au/grants-and-programs/industry-growth-program)

## 4. Self-Employment Assistance (federal) — not a fit

Allowance (~$809/fortnight) is for JobSeeker-equivalent-eligible founders and
requires availability to work the business — incompatible with FIFO income and
roster. Skip. ([dewr.gov.au](https://www.dewr.gov.au/self-employment-assistance))

## Notes

- WA's **Innovation Pathways Program** ($200k–$300k) funds accelerators and
  ecosystem providers, not startups directly — but the accelerators it funds
  are worth joining when one fits.
- Grant applications eat founder-nights. Strategy: **only the IBG justifies
  real effort**, timed for the ~March 2027 round with traction behind it.
  Everything else is opportunistic.
